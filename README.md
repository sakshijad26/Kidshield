
Full-stack vaccine management system with admin, frontend, and backend<br>

Objectives:<br>
●	Aadhaar-Based Child Registration : Ensure each child has a unique identity in the system using their Aadhaar number.<br>
●	Automated Vaccine Scheduling and Reminders:Automate the scheduling of vaccinations based on a child’s birth date and send timely reminders to parents.<br>
●	Vaccination Tracking: Accurate and real-time immunization coverage data to the authorities and health institutions in improving vaccination rates and reducing preventable diseases.<br>
●	Booking an Appointment:Allow parents to schedule vaccination appointments online to reduce waiting times at centers.<br>
●	Rescheduling or Cancelling an Appointment: Allow parents to modify or cancel appointments if needed.<br>
